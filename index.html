Welcome

This is the personal website of Jost Grant (or, Joshua Steven Grant)! Here, I share thoughts, projects (and related updates), and my journey more broadly as a Christian Futurist / Technologist; What that even means -- to me and hopefully help formalize what that means a bit more in-general.

(ESS' (CHATOLOGY))

Welcome to Chatology! This is a social space for Christian Futurists / Technologists. It's a cross-denominational community, with somewhat of a bias towards Pageauian styled Eastern-Orthodoxy; lol. I'm trying to cultivate people interested in Christian Symbolism, Symbolic Computing, and S-Expressions.

Rules Of Engagement

  • Don't be lewd, rude, or have an attitude.
  • Keep is safe for work, safe for church.
  • No spamming or self-promotion.
  • Keep discussions on-topic.
  • Follow the Discord Community Guidelines.

NOTE: Discord is not a long-term platform for us; For better-or-worse it's the best tool out there right now ... but there are major ideological differences / broader community issues I have with it -- and the plan is to eventually self-host everything. And there's a path to get there, but I don't see it happening until at the very earliest the tail-end of 2025.
